This project showcases my expertise in building custom WordPress solutions that are both functional and tailored to client requirements. Development of Custom Theme | Tokyo International JP

Wordpress Development

Tokyo International JP is a custom-built WordPress website designed specifically for car listings, aimed at creating a seamless platform for buyers and sellers in the automotive market. The site utilizes a dedicated custom post type to efficiently manage vehicle listings, making it easier for users to add, update, and view cars. One of the standout features of Tokyo International JP is the integration of Advanced Custom Fields (ACF). This allows for the inclusion of essential details in each listing, such as the make, model, year, mileage, price, and overall condition of the vehicle. This functionality enhances the user experience, providing potential buyers with all the necessary information at a glance. The design of the site prioritizes user experience, featuring a clean, modern layout that is fully responsive. This ensures that visitors can easily browse the listings on any device, whether it’s a desktop, tablet, or smartphone. Additionally, the site includes robust search and filter functionalities. Users can refine their search based on various criteria, including price range, make, model, and year, making it easier to find the perfect vehicle. Each listing also comes with a responsive image gallery, allowing sellers to showcase their cars effectively and attractively.
